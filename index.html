ABOUT

HISTORY

Twenty Pho Seven serves traditional Vietnamese food 24/7. It is vibrant, culture and hip hub to eat, drink and connect. Aside from the authentic aura it has, it also is a touch of modernity in it. This restaurant has been established by three college students from BCIT.

They though that having a pho while doing your web development homework at the middle of the night with your friends would be fun rather than just staying at home. After graduating, the three owners decided to build the pho place they just thought for fun.

Rather than being a typical pho place, they decide to put a little spice it in by making it pub-like. The first branch was first built near their alma matter however it was closed down. Today they have two branches; one in Richmond and one in East Vancouver.

MISSION

In Twenty Pho Seven, our mission is served guest authencity and modernity.

It is a place where they can hang out and chill with their family and friends

In Twenty Pho Seven, we want to provide a space for those students or people that is looking for a modern yet still relaxing environment

In Twenty pho seven, our mission is to support those locals and provide customers with food that is made with love and passion.
